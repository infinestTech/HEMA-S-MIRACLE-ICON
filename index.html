Sri Adichunchanagiri Women's College in Cumbum

On February 1, 2024, at 10:30 AM, a beauty therapy training class titled "INAUGURATION OF BEAUTY THERAPY" was conducted under the auspices of the administration of Sri Adisunchanagiri Women's College in Cumbum. The event took place in the presence of the college's Founder Secretary Mr. N. Ramakrishnan M.A., M.L.A., the Associate Secretary Mr. R. In his keynote address, the Founder Secretary spoke about internal beauty, external beauty, hair beauty, and the relationship between beauty and the body. Beauty therapy expert Ms. A. Hemasri discussed the nuances and importance of beauty therapy. Subsequently, classes commenced. Professors related to the field coordinated the beauty art event and the training.
